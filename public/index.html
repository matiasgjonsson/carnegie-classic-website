Weekend Overview

Friday, Feb 9 5pm-9pm Early Checkin (Optional)
7pm-10pm Welcome Dance: Starlit Soiree
Saturday, Feb 10 8am-6pm Matinee section: Newcomer, Bronze, Silver, Gold
7pm-11pm Special Surprise
Evening section: PreChamp and Open levels
Sunday, Feb 11 9am-5pm Dance Camp

All of the events will occur in Rangos Ballroom, which is located on
the second floor of the Jared L Cohon University Center at Carnegie Mellon University.

Free parking is available at the Morewood Gardens Parking Lot (entrance on Morewood Avenue) starting at 5pm on Friday evening.

A livestream link will be made available closer to the event.
