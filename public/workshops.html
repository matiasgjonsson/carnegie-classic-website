Maja Servé

World Professional Standard Finalist

Maja Servé is a distinguished five-time Danish Champion in Professional Standard. She has been a finalist in world-class competitions, including the World Championship, European Championship, British Open, International Championship, United Kingdom Championship, German Open, US Ballroom Championship, Japan Open, and Dutch Open. An IDTA Fellow in both Latin and Standard, and a NADTA Fellow in International Standard, International Latin, American Smooth, American Rhythm, and Theater Arts, Maja has also served as a judge representing the US in numerous international championships.
