Event Info

Carnegie Classic will run all day on Saturday, October 10, with wide-ranging levels and styles of dance being contested. There is also a social dance on Friday Night (see Starlit Soiree) and workshops on Sunday from morning till late afternoon.

The competition features seven levels for each dance style: Newcomer, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Novice, Pre-Championship, and Open.
- Matinee section (8am-6pm) Newcomer, Bronze, Silver, Gold
- Evening section (7pm-10pm) Pre-Champioship and Championship

Please see the Competitors page for a more detailed timetable. Live results will be posted during the competition at register.carnegieclassic.com
